Meet the Doctors

Dr. Katie W. Chu

Dr. Chu of TODAY…is a Woman of Strength and Resilience and a Person of Compassion and Empathy for ALL. She LOVES her family, friends, patients, and community and most of ALL, she LOVES life itself!!! Dr. Chu appreciates every blessing that comes into her life and loves seeing her patients because they give her joy every day. Dr. Chu is committed to returning that joy to her patients through kindness, respect, and care that they all deserve.

She has been practicing Optometry in the City of Rosemead since 1995. Her practice places a high priority on eye healthcare, contact lenses, premium products and services, and exceptional patient experience.

Dr. Chu is a first-generation American-born Chinese with deep family roots in this country starting with the Transcontinental Railway in 1864. For nearly 100 years, only the men from her family traveled to the US to find work and raise their families in China. Today, Dr. Chu is proud to celebrate 160 years of the true American Dream - families sacrificing to give their next generation better and safer lives.

Dr. Chu grew up in Monterey Park and attended Brightwood Elementary and Alhambra High. She received her Bachelor of Science degree from UCLA in Psychobiology and her Doctor of Optometry degree from Southern California College of Optometry.

During her externships, she worked with patients of diverse backgrounds while at Kaiser Permanente in Fontana and at the Optometric Center of Los Angeles located in South Central LA. She also gained experience while at the Naval Hospital and Marine Corp Recruiting Depot in San Diego where she treated both active and retired veterans and their families.

As a breast cancer survivor, Dr. Chu is keen on meeting the eye care needs of cancer patients. She is also able to relate to many of her patients with similar experiences through empathy and compassion.

Dr. Chu is exceptionally trained and experienced in contact lens fittings. She believes in the benefits that come from all her offerings, especially with the premium daily disposables for all ages and multifocal contact lens options for patients entering their Golden Years and beyond. Dr. Chu wants every patient to Live Better by Seeing Better and Feeling Better with the most advanced technology in contact lens material, optics, and solutions.

Over recent years, she has been invited by several major contact lens manufacturers to help with professional product reviews, which have been presented in highly regarded trade publications. She is an esteemed national speaker on contact lens and specialty products; sharing her contact lens expertise with other eyecare professionals across the country. She also provides valuable insight as a consultant at national sales meetings in the contact lens industry.

She has been co-managing LASIK patients for more than 20 years. She chooses to work with only the most experienced surgeons who use state-of-the-art technology.

For fun, Dr. Chu enjoys hosting parties of any size – from 4 to 104! And interior decorating always gives her pleasure. She loves the ocean, sunsets, and sunrises. She also loves ziplining, whitewater rafting, fishing, and camping. Dr Chu has traveled extensively over the years to almost 40 countries. Some of her favorite spots are Costa Rica, India, Italy, Vietnam, and the Galapagos Islands.

Dr. Kenneth W. Cao

Dr. Kenneth Cao was born and raised in the San Gabriel Valley. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from the University of California, Irvine, and his Doctor of Optometry degree from the Southern California College of Optometry in Fullerton, California.

Pursuing his passion for education and advanced clinical training, Dr. Cao completed his residency at the Veterans Affairs Southern Nevada Healthcare System. Dr. Cao is honored and thrilled to serve alongside Dr. Katie Chu and her team as an associate doctor.

About Us

Dr. Katie W. Chu has a team of caring members who are simply there to give the best care for each patient and to meet each patient's individual needs. We take pride that our practice has another optometrist who works with Dr. Chu in caring for patients.

The family-like quality of our practice is also attributed to one of the lead staff members working with Dr. Chu since 1997. It is a wonderful feeling to walk into our office year after year with the same people helping you.

Our practice provides comprehensive eye exams for glasses and contact lenses. We have a wide selection of eyewear and lens designs to meet your visual needs.

We also work with local medical specialists to help diagnose, treat and manage various eye conditions. We are also an affiliated optometry practice of TLC Laser Eye Centers. Our relationship with them for the past 20 years have solidified our experience in co-managing our LASIK laser eye surgery patients before and after the life changing procedure.
